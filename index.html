The problem I spotted

Great reach, quiet mornings.

Posts blew up at 8pm — long after the café had closed. Lots of love, but it wasn't turning into weekday footfall.

The fix I made

Moved the money posts to 7:30am.

Shifted "cosy morning" content to breakfast hours + added a weekday-only offer. Result: +27 weekday walk-ins the very next month.
